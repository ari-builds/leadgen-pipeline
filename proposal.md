# NetClicks by Ari + AI Conversion Suite
## Combined Lead Generation & Conversion Proposal

---

## The Problem

Service-based businesses lose money in two places:

1. **Not enough leads coming in** — they rely on word-of-mouth, referrals, and hope
2. **Leads that do come in go cold** — missed calls, slow responses, no follow-up system

Most businesses fix one or the other. Rarely both.

---

## The Solution: Full-Funnel AI Pipeline

### NetClicks by Ari (Demand Generation)
- AI-powered lead scraping from 10+ sources (Google, Facebook, LinkedIn, obituaries, review sites, community forums)
- Behavioral scoring (1-10) based on intent signals
- Personalized outreach angles for every lead
- Real-time client dashboard with analytics
- Monthly lead delivery with export options

### AI Conversion Suite (Conversion & Follow-Up)
- AI chatbot for website — answers FAQs, qualifies leads, books appointments 24/7
- AI call agent — picks up missed calls, captures lead details, schedules appointments after hours
- Automated follow-up workflows — email, SMS, WhatsApp sequences triggered by lead behavior
- Booking integration — connects with existing calendar/booking platforms

## Why This Works for Service Businesses

- **They're busy working** — they can't answer every call or follow up with every inquiry. The AI handles it.
- **Their customers expect instant responses** — 78% of customers go with the first business that responds. Speed wins.
- **They're already paying for marketing** — this replaces fragmented tools with one integrated system.
- **The ROI is obvious** — more leads in + higher conversion rate = more customers without more ad spend.
