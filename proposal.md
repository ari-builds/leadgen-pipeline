## The Problem

Most service-based businesses — HVAC, plumbing, dental, legal, home services, memorial services — are bleeding money in two directions:

1. **They don't have enough qualified leads coming in.** They're either not marketing, or their marketing only generates phone numbers and form fills, not actual demand.
2. **They lose leads on the back end.** Even the leads they do get fall through the cracks because nobody responds fast enough, follow-up is manual and inconsistent, and the business owner is too busy running the business to chase every inquiry.

They're paying for marketing that only solves half the problem, then duct-taping together a bunch of separate tools for the other half — and most of the time those tools don't talk to each other.

---

## What We're Building

One provider. One system. Full pipeline from first contact to booked appointment.

- **Demand Generation:** I find the people who are already looking for your services but haven't called you yet. I score them based on how likely they are to convert, give you the reasoning behind each score, and hand off qualified leads to your team.
- **Conversion Infrastructure:** The moment a prospect engages — replies to an outreach email, clicks a link, fills out a form, or calls in — your AI system picks up instantly. Chatbot qualifies them on the website. Call agent captures them by phone. Follow-up workflows nurture the ones who don't convert immediately.
- **Closed-Loop Results:** A single dashboard shows the full picture — from lead generation through conversion to appointment. Everything connected. Nothing falls through the cracks.

---

## How The Handoff Works

My system identifies and qualifies leads, then initiates personalized outreach. That outreach drives prospects to the client's website or phone line.

The moment a prospect engages, your side picks up:

- **Website visitors** → AI chatbot qualifies them, captures details, recommends the right service, and walks them through booking
- **Phone calls** → AI call agent handles inquiries, captures lead info, books appointments — especially valuable for field service owners who are on a job all day
- **Follow-up** → Automated sequences based on behavior. Lead came in but didn't book? Sequence kicks in. Lead booked but didn't show? Re-engagement sequence.

Since you integrate with existing platforms like Mindbody, Calendly, and Google Calendar, the booking handoff is smooth — the client keeps whatever system they're already using.

The connection point between both systems is the key:

- Your system needs to know when a lead from my pipeline engages so it can personalize the conversation
- My system needs to know when a lead converts so the dashboard updates and we stop following up with someone who already booked

That's the technical piece we'd wire together during the pilot.
